## Executive Leadership

- Client relationship management
- Performance oversight

**Interactions:**
- Receives reports from VP Operations
- Approves marketing campaigns

- Oversees day-to-day operations

## Agent Personality System

### Personality Traits
Each agent has a unique personality profile affecting:

**Work Ethic:**
- Diligent: High productivity, attention to detail
- Perfectionist: Quality-focused, may be slow
- Efficient: Fast but may cut corners
- Lazy: Low productivity, needs motivation

**Communication Style:**
- Direct: Concise, to-the-point
- Verbose: Detailed explanations
- Collaborative: Seeks input from others
- Independent: Prefers solo work

**Social Dynamics:**
- Team Player: Helpful, collaborative
- Competitive: Driven, may be territorial
- Mentor: Teaches and guides others
- Reclusive: Prefers minimal interaction

### Personality Impact
- **Task Assignment:** Match tasks to personality strengths
- **Team Dynamics:** Balance personality types for optimal collaboration
- **Performance:** Personality affects work quality and speed
- **Communication:** Different approaches to problem-solving

## Department Interactions

### Engineering ↔ Sales
- Sales provides requirements and feedback
- Engineering delivers features and updates
- Regular demos and status meetings

### Engineering ↔ QA
- QA reviews code and finds bugs
- Engineering fixes issues and improves quality
- Automated testing integration

### Sales ↔ Marketing
- Marketing generates leads for sales
- Sales provides customer insights to marketing
- Coordinated campaign execution

### All Departments ↔ Management
- Daily/weekly status reports
- Resource requests and approvals
- Strategic planning input

## Communication Protocols

### Formal Communications
- **Reports:** Structured status updates to managers
- **Meetings:** Scheduled discussions with agendas
- **Documentation:** Requirements, designs, and decisions

### Informal Communications
- **Water Cooler:** Casual agent-to-agent conversations
- **Help Requests:** Seeking assistance from specialists
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Best practices and lessons learned

### Emergency Communications
- **System Outages:** Immediate notifications to all affected
- **Security Issues:** Rapid response coordination
- **Client Emergencies:** Priority handling and updates
